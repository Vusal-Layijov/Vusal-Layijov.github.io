Who I am...

Hi! and... Welcome! My name is Vusal Layijov. I was born in Balakan, Azerbaijan and graduated from Azerbaijan State Oil Academy with a BA in Business Administration. I worked in the banking sector for a while, but I decided to check my luck and apply for a green card, which I won. I moved to the US, the birthplace of technology, and started learning how to repair electronic devices, which has always been my passion. I am highly interested in technology and love learning new skills. During my four-year period of hardware repair, I also worked on some software issues. With my never-ending passion for learning, I started coding. I attended App Academy and learned key software engineering skills. I am able to work well both independently and as a part of a productive team, as well as under pressure. I am currently improving my developer skills and looking for new job opportunities as a software engineer. Apart from work, I enjoy watching superhero movies (which is why I aspire to be a super developer), traveling, reading, going to the gym, and constantly improving myself, as Bruce Wayne once said, "You can always better yourself."
